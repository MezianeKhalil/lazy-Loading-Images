lazy loading images

Images are amongst the most common culprits of poor performance in websites,this can have a huge impact on load times and potentially make the website unusable. To get around this, developers typically resort to lazy loading, a technique that comprises of sending a tiny temporary image with the initial payload, In practice, this means that users don’t have to wait for a large asset to be downloaded before they start seeing content on the screen, which is an improvement in perceived performance.
